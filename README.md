# Jenu-Gumpu – Honey Producer Collective App

## Overview

Jenu-Gumpu is an Android application developed to support tribal and rural honey hunters by helping them manage honey collection, grading, stock tracking, and profit estimation. The app focuses on agriculture and self-employment by enabling honey producers to improve product quality, maintain records, and understand market pricing.

The application aims to reduce dependency on middlemen and empower honey hunters to sell their products at better prices.

---

# Problem Statement

Many tribal and rural honey hunters sell raw honey at very low prices because they:

* Do not know the actual retail market value
* Lack knowledge of honey grading and quality checking
* Do not maintain stock records
* Have no proper batch tracking system
* Depend heavily on middlemen

Jenu-Gumpu solves these issues through a simple Android application.

---

* Help honey producers maintain harvest records

# Features

## 1. Harvest Log

Users can store:

* Harvest date
* Collection location
* Quantity of honey collected

This helps maintain proper collection history.

---

## 2. Honey Grading Tool

The app provides a simple grading guide based on:

* Honey color
* Moisture level
* Quality indicators

This helps users understand the quality of collected honey.

---

## 3. Price Monitor

Users can compare:

* Wholesale prices
* Retail prices

This helps honey producers negotiate better prices.

---

## 4. Batch Tracking

Each honey collection batch can be assigned a unique Batch ID for:

* Product tracking
* Stock management
* Traceability

---

## 5. Profit Calculator

The app estimates profit after processing costs.

Example:

Selling Price – Filtering Cost = Profit
